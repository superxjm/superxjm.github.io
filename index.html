Jiamin Xu (许佳敏)

Lecturer, Hangzhou Dianzi University

superxjm at yeah dot net

I am currently with the College of Computer Science and Technology, Hangzhou Dianzi University, China. I received the Ph.D. degree in the State Key Lab of CAD&CG, Zhejiang University, supervised by Prof. Hujun Bao and Prof. WeiWei Xu. Before that, I received the B.Eng. and M.S. degree from the Shandong University of Science and Technology, Nanjing University respectively.

My research focuses on 3D Reconstruction, Neural Rendering, Image-based Rendering.
